Black Galaxy Granite


Black galaxy granite is in the gabbro family of stone material and is sourced in the Ongole area of South India. The granite material is rated a 4.5 on the Moh’s scale of hardness, which is considered relatively hard.

There are harder building materials, such as quartz, but granite is durable enough to withstand most common use.

Specifications

"Durability"

Tough material that can take daily use and doesn’t require heavy maintenance. When used in high-traffic areas, though, scratching is possible. 


Dust, sand, and debris can all cause scuffs and scratches that might show prominently on the dark-colored surface.

Usage

Galaxy granite is most commonly used in flooring, staircases, and countertops.


The durability and beauty of our material are traits that usually put it in an area that is highly trafficked and will be seen by many
